Tajweed is an Arabic-first educational app focused on Tajweed rules from
Tuhfat Al-Atfal.

The app helps students study rules, practice interactively, and review progress
over time.

Core features:

* Structured lessons grouped by section and rule
* Rule details with explanations, examples, and verses from the poem
* Multiple practice types:
  * Multiple Choice
  * True / False
  * Letter Match
  * Section Match
* Practice by all rules, specific section, or specific rule
* Timed and question-count practice modes
* Results view with mistake review and explanations
* Analysis dashboard for strengths, weaknesses, and recent attempts
* Local on-device storage of practice history

Optional online mode:

* Question data can be loaded from Firestore if enabled
* Manual content management from Firebase Console
* Automatic offline fallback if online data is unavailable

Designed for learners who want a clean, modern Arabic UI and practical Tajweed
training flow.
